Surveys have shown that 3 out of every 5 Americans are
experiencing loneliness - an astounding 13% rise since 2018.

Bring people together in real life to enjoy the things they usually do alone. We’re creating a platform for those who genuinely want to enjoy life with someone from time to time with no pressure to invite them over. And ultimately, we’re making the world a friendlier place for everyone – one connection at a time.

A radical new approach to social media

Social media is usually designed to grab your attention and keep you scrolling or swiping through content or profiles that interest you like a bug addicted to a light.

But Bridge is pioneering a new kind of social media by enabling you to create the kind of real-life, no-pressure scenario where genuine connections can be made.
